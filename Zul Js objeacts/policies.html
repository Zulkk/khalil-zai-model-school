Khalil Zai Model School Policies

Ensuring a safe, disciplined, and productive learning environment

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is mandatory for academic success.

  • Students must maintain 85% attendance to sit for exams.
  • Absences require a written excuse from parents/guardians.
  • Medical leave requires a doctor's certificate.
  • Late arrivals (after 8:15 AM) will be marked as half-day absent.

Discipline Policy

We maintain a strict code of conduct for all students.

  • Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students is mandatory.
  • Bullying, fighting, or vandalism will result in suspension.
  • Use of mobile phones is prohibited during school hours.
  • Repeated violations may lead to expulsion.

Uniform Policy

Proper uniform is required for all students.

  • Winter & summer uniforms must be worn as prescribed.
  • No jewelry, colored hair, or makeup allowed.
  • Sports uniform only on designated days.
  • Non-compliance will result in disciplinary action.

Fee Policy

Timely fee payment ensures uninterrupted education.

  • Tuition fees due by the 10th of each month.
  • A 5% late fee applies after the due date.
  • Non-payment for two months may lead to suspension.
  • Admission & registration fees are non-refundable.

Examination Policy

Fair and transparent assessment procedures.

  • Two mid-terms and one final exam per year.
  • Cheating will result in a zero score.
  • Re-exams only for students with valid medical reasons.
  • Results are final and cannot be challenged.

Parental Involvement

Parents play a key role in student success.

  • Mandatory attendance at PTM (Parent-Teacher Meetings).
  • Check student diaries daily for updates.
  • Notify school of any concerns immediately.
  • Volunteer opportunities available for school events.
